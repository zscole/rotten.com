World's richest man skips out on coffee bill
AZ Central | Submitted by: Tim Howard
"Microsoft Corp. co-founder Bill Gates came to Watertown to talk to librarians about computers that the Bill and Melinda Gates Foundation had donated. They should just do what Microsoft does: chalk it up to the cost of doing business and not make a big deal out of it.
