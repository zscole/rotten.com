Schwarzenegger told campaign aides this week that he does not recall earning a salary during his first year in America, even though he wrote about it in his autobiography and the arrangement has been reported in accounts over the decades. ... Schwarzenegger declined to be interviewed or release immigration records that detail his employment history in the United States."
